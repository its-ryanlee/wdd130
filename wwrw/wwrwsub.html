Q's and A's

Q. What is white water rafting?

A. White water rafting, also known as river rafting, is an exhilarating outdoor activity where participants navigate through rough waters on inflatable rafts. It's a thrilling adventure sport enjoyed by individuals and groups seeking excitement and adrenaline amidst the beauty of nature.

Q. What equipment do I need for white water rafting?

A. Typically, participants are provided with safety gear including helmets, life jackets, and paddles by the rafting outfitter. Comfortable clothing suitable for water activities, such as swimwear or quick-dry clothing, is recommended. You may also need water shoes or sandals with secure straps to protect your feet.

Q. Do I need prior experience to go white water rafting?

A. No prior experience is required for most white water rafting trips. Rafting companies offer trips suitable for various skill levels, from beginner-friendly to advanced. Experienced guides provide instructions on paddling techniques and safety protocols before embarking on the adventure.
